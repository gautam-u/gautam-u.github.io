Privacy Policy

Workbill is built to minimize collection. The app is usable without creating an account, and your work logs are intended to stay on your device unless you opt into Apple-provided syncing features.

What Workbill stores

  • Projects, billable sessions, rates, and invoice-related data you create in the app.
  • Purchase state needed to unlock premium features through Apple's in-app purchase system.
  • Optional Apple ecosystem sync data if you enable iCloud-related features.

What Workbill does not require

  • No mandatory account registration.
  • No advertising profile.
  • No selling of personal data.

Support contact

If you contact support, your email and the details you send are used only to respond to your request.
